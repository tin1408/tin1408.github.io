Welcome to Research Lab

We are a research group at the Faculty of Computer Science & Engineering, Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT). Our research spans Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Artificial Intelligence with real-world applications.

Research Areas

🖼️

Computer Vision

Image recognition, object detection, semantic segmentation, and 3D scene understanding using deep neural networks.

🤖

Machine Learning

Novel learning paradigms including self-supervised learning, few-shot learning, and continual learning for real-world applications.

💬

Natural Language Processing

Language models, information extraction, machine translation, and multimodal understanding combining vision and language.

🏥

AI for Healthcare

Medical image analysis, disease diagnosis assistance, and clinical decision support using AI technologies.

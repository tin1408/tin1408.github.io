Openings

We welcome passionate students and researchers who are interested in joining our lab. Please read the requirements carefully before reaching out.

 PhD Students

We have openings for PhD students starting Fall 2025 / Spring 2026. Candidates with a strong background in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, or NLP are encouraged to apply.

  • B.Sc. or M.Sc. in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or a related field
  • Strong mathematical background (linear algebra, probability, optimization)
  • Programming proficiency in Python and PyTorch / TensorFlow
  • Prior research experience or publications is a plus

Fellowship: Full scholarship (stipend + tuition) via NAFOSTED / VINIF / HCMUT programs. See fellowship details. Deadline: August 31, 2025.

Apply for PhD

 Master Students

We accept master students each semester. Research master positions are available with partial funding support.

  • B.Sc. in Computer Science or related field (HCMUT graduates preferred)
  • GPA ≥ 7.5/10 (or equivalent)
  • Proficient in Python and basic deep learning frameworks
Apply for Master

 Research Interns & Undergraduate Researchers

We regularly host undergraduate research interns (3-6 months). Interns work closely with PhD students on ongoing projects.

  • Currently enrolled at HCMUT or a top Vietnamese university
  • Available for at least 20 hours/week
  • Basic knowledge of Python and machine learning
  • Self-motivated and able to work independently
Apply for Internship

 How to Apply

Please send an email to pi@hcmut.edu.vn with the subject line [Position] Your Name. Include: (1) your CV, (2) transcripts, (3) a brief statement of research interest (1 page), and (4) any relevant publications or projects. We will respond within 2 weeks if your profile matches our current needs.
